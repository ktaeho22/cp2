Bio
Fer Jane is an one man band found by John Doe from Provo, Utah. Since April 2020, John decided to follow his passion. The year 2020 is a mess and to make the world a little better, he decided to do it by sharing his musical talents. Since then, he traveled around Utah wherever the venue is on a street or in a bar, he started to sing.
